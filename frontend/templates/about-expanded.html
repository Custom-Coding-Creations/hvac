Our Story: 80+ Years of Excellence

Since 1944, Potter Heating & Air Conditioning and Perrone Plumbing have been serving Syracuse and Central New York with trusted HVAC and plumbing services. What started as a commitment to quality has become a legacy of excellence.

Our Journey Through the Decades

1944

The Foundation

Potter Heating & Air Conditioning and Perrone Plumbing are founded, establishing a commitment to quality heating, cooling, and plumbing services for Syracuse families and businesses.

1950s-1980s

Growth & Expansion

Decades of consistent service build a reputation for reliability and expertise. The company grows to serve more homes and commercial clients across Central New York.

1990s-2000s

Modernization

As technology evolves, Potter-Perrone stays at the forefront with modern HVAC systems, plumbing innovations, and customer service excellence.

2010s-Present

Leadership & Innovation

Under Suzanne Kondra-DeFuria's leadership, the company combines 80+ years of tradition with modern service standards and a commitment to the community.

Our Mission
