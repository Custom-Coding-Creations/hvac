Emergency Service Guide

Know what to do when HVAC or plumbing emergencies strike. Quick action can prevent damage and get you help faster.

🔥 Heating Emergency

  1. Check thermostat settings (Heat mode, temperature set appropriately)
  2. Reset the system by switching off for 30 seconds, then back on
  3. Check breaker - flip back on if tripped
  4. If still not working: Call (315) 472-3557 immediately

❄️ Cooling Emergency

  1. Check thermostat is set to Cool mode at proper temperature
  2. Verify outdoor unit has power (listen for fan operation)
  3. Check indoor air filter - dirty filters reduce efficiency
  4. If still not working: Call (315) 472-3557 immediately

💧 Plumbing Emergency

  1. Locate main water shut-off valve
  2. Turn OFF water at main shut-off immediately
  3. Open faucets to drain remaining water
  4. Then call: (315) 472-3557 for emergency repair

🌡️ Water Heater Issue

  1. Check temperature setting (usually 120°F is recommended)
  2. Verify pilot light is lit (gas heaters)
  3. Check breaker hasn't tripped (electric heaters)
  4. If leaking: Turn off at shut-off valve and call us

For any emergency, call (315) 472-3557 immediately
