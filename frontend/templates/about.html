About Our Company

Your Dependable, Trustworthy Plumbing & HVAC Expert

Since 1944, Potter Heating and Air Conditioning & Perrone Plumbing has been serving the Syracuse area with exceptional heating, cooling, and plumbing services. Our Heritage

Founded in 1944, Potter Heating and Air Conditioning & Perrone Plumbing has been a cornerstone of the Syracuse community for more than eight decades. What started as a family business has grown into a full-service HVAC and plumbing provider known for reliability, integrity, and outstanding customer service.

We're not just a residential contractor. We proudly serve residential customers, commercial facilities, and institutional clients throughout Central New York with the same commitment to excellence that has defined us since our founding.
