Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my furnace serviced?

We recommend annual maintenance before each heating season. This keeps your system running efficiently and prevents costly emergency repairs.

What's the best time to schedule AC maintenance?

Spring is ideal to prepare for summer. Schedule a tune-up in April or May to ensure your system is ready for the hot months ahead.

How long do water heaters typically last?

Tank water heaters usually last 8-12 years. If yours is showing age or reducing hot water, we can assess whether repair or replacement is best.

What should I do if a pipe freezes?

Call us immediately at (315) 472-3557. We provide emergency service and can thaw frozen pipes safely and quickly.
