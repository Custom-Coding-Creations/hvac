# Syracuse Website Prospect Scoring Rubric

Use this rubric to rank local small-business redesign opportunities for a $2k+ full rebuild offer.

## Gate Conditions

Disqualify a prospect if any of these are true:
- No on-site email and no contact form
- Business appears inactive or closed
- Website is already modern, mobile-friendly, and conversion-aware
- Business looks too small or price-sensitive for a $2k+ project

## Weighted Score

Total score = outdated severity (35) + demand (25) + budget fit (25) + contactability (15)

Maximum total: 100
Target outreach threshold: 65+
Priority threshold: 75+

## 1. Outdated Severity (0-35)

Score each item from 0-5, then sum.

- Mobile UX friction
  - 0: clean responsive layout
  - 3: usable but cramped or inconsistent
  - 5: visibly broken or hard to use on mobile
- Page speed friction
  - 0: fast
  - 3: noticeably slow
  - 5: very slow or blocking assets
- Stale content / maintenance neglect
  - 0: current
  - 3: last meaningful update is old
  - 5: obvious stale dates, broken content, outdated copyright
- CTA weakness
  - 0: clear conversion path
  - 3: weak or buried CTA
  - 5: no strong next step
- Trust signal weakness
  - 0: reviews, credentials, staff, service proof shown clearly
  - 3: partial trust content
  - 5: weak credibility presentation
- Design age / UX debt
  - 0: modern
  - 3: dated
  - 5: clearly outdated
- Technical quality issues
  - 0: no obvious issues
  - 3: minor problems
  - 5: broken links, mixed content, intrusive popups, poor formatting

Cap this section at 35.

## 2. Demand (0-25)

- Third-party traffic tier
  - 0: no evidence / negligible
  - 3: low but nonzero
  - 5: meaningful local traffic
  - 8: strong traffic signal for a local business
- Local keyword visibility
  - 0: weak or absent
  - 3: ranks for some branded terms only
  - 5: visible for useful local service terms
  - 8: strong visibility across several local terms
- Review velocity and business activity
  - 0: inactive
  - 3: occasional activity
  - 5: active recent reviews
  - 7: strong ongoing review flow or active promotion
- Directory / ad presence
  - 0: none
  - 1: one signal present
  - 2: multiple demand signals present

Cap this section at 25.

## 3. Budget Fit (0-25)

- Business maturity
  - 0: unclear or fragile
  - 3: somewhat established
  - 5: clearly established
- Team / scale signal
  - 0: solo or unclear
  - 3: small team
  - 5: established team or multi-provider business
- Marketing spend signal
  - 0: none visible
  - 3: some investment
  - 5: ads, strong branding, professional assets, or active promotions
- Revenue fit by vertical
  - 0: low-margin or highly price-sensitive
  - 3: average
  - 5: strong service economics
- Competitive pressure
  - 0: no clear reason to upgrade
  - 2: some peer pressure
  - 5: clear gap versus stronger local competitors

Cap this section at 25.

## 4. Contactability (0-15)

- On-site email
  - 0: absent
  - 5: present
- Contact form
  - 0: absent
  - 4: present
- Phone number
  - 0: absent
  - 3: present
- Clear owner / team identity
  - 0: absent
  - 3: present

Cap this section at 15.

## Suggested Workflow

1. Build a raw list of 20-30 businesses in one Syracuse vertical.
2. Disqualify obvious non-fits fast.
3. Score the remaining sites in under 5 minutes each.
4. Sort by total score.
5. Write one custom outreach hook for the top 5.
6. Send outreach in small batches and record replies.

## Best First Verticals for Syracuse

- Law firms
- Dentists and specialty clinics
- HVAC, roofing, plumbing, landscaping
- Real estate teams and brokerages
- Wedding venues and event businesses
- Established accounting and financial services
