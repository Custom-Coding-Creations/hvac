## Appendix: Meeting Guidelines

### Pre-Meeting Preparation (24 hours before)
- [ ] Analytics Lead updates KPI dashboard
- [ ] All owners submit status updates
- [ ] Facilitator reviews risk register and decision backlog
- [ ] Note taker confirms availability

### Meeting Flow
1. **Opening (2 min):** Quick round of status from each owner
2. **KPIs (15 min):** Review dashboard, discuss trends, identify actions
3. **Delivery (10 min):** Progress review, upcoming work, dependencies
4. **Risks (10 min):** Blocker triage, risk review, new risk identification
5. **Decisions (5 min):** Decision review, voting if needed, documentation
6. **Escalations (5 min):** Active escalation status, new escalations
7. **Actions (5 min):** Action item review, assignments, deadlines
8. **Closing (3 min):** Next steps, milestone reminders, feedback

### Decision-Making Protocol
- **Consensus preferred:** All owners agree on path forward
- **Program Lead breaks ties:** If consensus not reached, Program Lead decides
- **Program Sponsor veto:** Sponsor can override on strategic/budget decisions
- **Escalation threshold:** If decision impacts timeline >1 week or budget >10%, escalate to Sponsor

### Parking Lot Protocol
- Items requiring >5 minutes of discussion are moved to parking lot
- Parking lot items scheduled for dedicated follow-up within 48 hours
- Facilitator assigns owner and due date for each parking lot item

### Action Item Protocol
- All action items have specific owner (single name, not team)
- All action items have specific due date (not "next week" or "soon")
- Owner confirms acceptance of action item before meeting ends
- Overdue action items trigger automatic escalation after 2 business days
