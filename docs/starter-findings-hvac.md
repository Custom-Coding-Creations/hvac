# Starter Findings: Syracuse HVAC

This file captures the first live checks from the Syracuse HVAC vertical.

## Likely Opportunity

### Potter Heating & Air Conditioning-Perrone Plumbing
- Website: https://potter-perrone.com/
- Why it stands out:
  - 78 Google reviews
  - Established since 1944
  - Direct email on site: service@potter-perrone.com
  - Contact page and phone number on site
  - Offers residential, commercial, and federal/institutional services
  - Financing available
- Why it looks promising for a redesign pitch:
  - Site appears visually dated and cluttered
  - Trust and service breadth are present but not presented cleanly
  - Older structure likely makes mobile conversion and scanning weaker than it should be
  - Good example of a business with real commercial maturity but older web presentation
- Suggested hook:
  - Their website does not present a modern, high-trust path for a business with decades of history and multiple service lines.

### Spencer Heating & Air Conditioning
- Website: https://www.spencerheating.com/
- Why it is worth keeping on the watchlist:
  - 18 Google reviews
  - 84 years in business
  - Contact page and phone number on site
  - Financing and emergency service are visible
- Why it is weaker than Potter:
  - Lower visible demand signal
  - Contactability is weaker because on-site email was not confirmed
  - Site is dated, but the business signal is not as strong as Potter's
- Suggested next check:
  - Confirm whether the business is running ads or ranking strongly enough to justify outreach now.

## Non-Target Example
