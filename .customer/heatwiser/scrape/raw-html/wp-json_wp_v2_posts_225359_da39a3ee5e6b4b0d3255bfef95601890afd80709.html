    What to Expect During a Spring HVAC Tune-Up<\/h2>\r\n

Here\u2019s what that includes:<\/span><\/p>\r\n

    Outdoor Unit Maintenance<\/strong><\/h3>\r\n

    Your outdoor unit has been exposed to months of Central New York\u2019s harsh winter weather. We\u2019ll make sure it\u2019s clean, clear, and running efficiently by:<\/span><\/p>\r\n

      \r\n
    • Checking refrigerant levels to make sure they are at the manufacturing level, which helps keep your system cooling properly<\/span><\/li>\r\n
    • Inspect the base pan and drain openings (if accessible) to ensure proper drainage and remove any blockages<\/span><\/li>\r\n
    • Cleaning the coil and cabinet to enhance performance and boost energy efficiency<\/span><\/li>\r\n
    • Examining the fan motor and blades for signs of wear or damage<\/span><\/li>\r\n
    • Cleaning the outdoor condensing unit as needed<\/span><\/li>\r\n
    • Checking amp draws to ensure components are operating within proper ranges<\/span><\/li>\r\n
    • Checking all electrical connections on the outdoor unit<\/span><\/li>\r\n
    • Testing the capacitor for proper function<\/span><\/li>\r\n<\/ul>\r\n

      Indoor Unit Maintenance<\/b><\/h3>\r\n

      Inside your home, we dig into the details to make sure your system is clean, safe, and ready to go:<\/span><\/p>\r\n

        \r\n
      • Checking the blower assembly, including the housing, wheel, and motor<\/span><\/li>\r\n
      • Checking motors and fan belts on older units to prevent wear and tear<\/span><\/li>\r\n
      • Checking the evaporator coil, drain pan, and condensate lines (if accessible), which helps prevent clogs and water damage<\/span><\/li>\r\n
      • Checking for gas leaks<\/span><\/a> and inspecting heat exchanger in gas furnace systems to ensure your family\u2019s safety<\/span><\/li>\r\n
      • Inspecting and adjusting the burner assembly, if needed<\/span><\/li>\r\n
      • Examining the control box, wiring, and electrical connections for safe and reliable performance<\/span><\/li>\r\n
      • Inspecting air filters<\/a> to improve air quality and system efficiency<\/li>\r\n<\/ul>\r\n
