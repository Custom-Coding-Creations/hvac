1. Beat the Heat with Energy-Efficient Cooling<\/b><\/h2>\n

The first few weeks of school can still bring plenty of <\/span>warm Central New York afternoons<\/span><\/a>. Your air conditioner needs to work efficiently without running up your utility bills.\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

Here\u2019s how to save energy and stay cool:<\/b><\/p>\n

    \n
  • Upgrade to a smart thermostat:<\/b> These handy devices let you program cooling schedules around when your family is home. No more cooling an empty house while everyone is at school or work.<\/span><\/li>\n
  • Set your thermostat wisely:<\/b> Aim for <\/span>78\u00b0F when home<\/b> and bump it up a few degrees when away. Every degree you raise your thermostat can <\/span>save up to 3% on cooling costs.<\/span><\/a><\/li>\n
  • Check your air filters:<\/b> A clogged filter <\/span><\/a>makes your system work harder. Replacing filters monthly during peak cooling season keeps airflow strong and energy bills low.<\/span><\/li>\n
  • Close blinds and curtains:<\/b> Keep the afternoon sun from heating up the house, especially in kids\u2019 bedrooms and play areas.<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Pro Tip:<\/b> Want a customized efficiency boost? <\/span>Ask our Heat Wiser team about upgrading<\/span><\/a> to a high-efficiency air conditioner or adding zoning to target cooling where your family needs it most.<\/span><\/p>\n

    2. Keep Kids Comfortable at Home<\/b><\/h2>\n

    A comfortable home makes all the difference when kids are trying to concentrate on homework or get a good night\u2019s sleep.\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

    Here\u2019s how to create the perfect indoor environment for your family:<\/b><\/p>\n

      \n
    • Maintain consistent temperatures:<\/b> Keeping thermostat settings steady prevents system strain and ensures comfort from room to room.<\/span><\/li>\n
    • Improve indoor air quality: <\/b>With more allergens and germs in the air, Heat Wiser\u2019s <\/span>purification solutions<\/span><\/a> help keep your home clean and your family healthy.<\/span><\/li>\n
    • Use ceiling fans wisely:<\/b> Running fans clockwise in the summer pushes cool air down, helping kids feel cooler without cranking the AC.<\/span><\/li>\n
    • Quiet comfort matters:<\/b> Strange noises<\/span><\/a> from your HVAC system can be distracting during study time. If you hear clanking, rattling, or buzzing, it\u2019s time to <\/span>call in a pro.<\/span><\/a><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

      Between school drop-offs, soccer practice, and grocery runs, scheduling HVAC service may feel impossible, but it doesn\u2019t have to be. Preventative maintenance is the best way to avoid breakdowns when your family is relying most on comfort.<\/span><\/p>\n

        \n
      • Book early:<\/b> Fall is the perfect time to<\/span> schedule your annual heating tune-up<\/span><\/a> before the first chilly night hits.<\/span><\/li>\n

      • Think long-term savings:<\/b> A simple tune-up can improve efficiency by up to 15% and extend your system\u2019s lifespan, which means fewer surprises and <\/span>lower repair bills.<\/span><\/a><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
