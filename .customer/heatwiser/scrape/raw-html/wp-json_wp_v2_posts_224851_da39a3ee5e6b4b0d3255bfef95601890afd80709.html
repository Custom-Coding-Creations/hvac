Spring and summer are notorious for stirring up allergies in Liverpool. If you\u2019re one of the people who suffer from hay fever or are sensitive to allergens in general, know that there\u2019s something you can do. In fact, the solution is already sitting in your home: your HVAC system!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Keep on reading to learn what to do with your HVAC system in Liverpool if you have allergies.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Fresh filters<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

You obviously know that your HVAC\u2019s filters\u2026 filter everything from dust to pollutants. But it\u2019s really much more than that. As air from outside your Liverpool home passes through it, allergens like dust, pollen, and animal hair get caught in the filter, preventing it from getting into your home. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

But filters only work if they\u2019re clean. If you don\u2019t regularly clean out your filters, however, the trapped allergens can be pushed into your home, triggering your allergies. It can also cause your HVAC system to conk out sooner rather than later because the system has to work double time just to pass air through the unit.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We recommend replacing your filters at least once a month if you\u2019re prone to allergies. You can also clean it every week to make it last longer.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Clean vents<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Vents have lots of tiny moving parts, making it susceptible to gathering dust. Cleaning it is easy. Just grab a microfiber cloth and gently wipe it over the vents. A clean vent also gives your filters less work to do!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Clear air ducts<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Another piece you need to regularly clean is your Liverpool HVAC system\u2019s airducts. When allergens and debris get caught in them, it can lead to poor air quality in your home, causing you to sneeze or cough. What\u2019s worse is that if you ignore your air ducts, it can become home to dangerous contaminants like mold, mildew, and pollen.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Make sure you schedule regular HVAC repair in Liverpool, NY at least once per quarter to make sure your air ducts are squeaky clean.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

A clean outside HVAC unit<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

A lot of people tend to forget to clean the outside unit of their HVAC system. This is a big mistake! HVAC systems pull air from outside your Liverpool property, cleans and cools it, then pushes it into your home. Make it a habit to check your HVAC system for any debris like leaves, dust, and even branches that may have gotten stuck on the outside unit.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Put Your HVAC System to Work<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

It\u2019s no secret that allergens live in the air we breathe. If your HVAC system is clean and well-maintained, it can filter out allergens, preventing an attack from happening. To reduce your symptoms of an allergic reaction, we recommend shutting your doors and windows and to just let your HVAC system do its thing!<\/p>\n\n\n\n
