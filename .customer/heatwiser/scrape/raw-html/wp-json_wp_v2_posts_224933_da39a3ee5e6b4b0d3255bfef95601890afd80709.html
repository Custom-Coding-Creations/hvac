If you live in New York and share your home with pets, it’s important to take some extra steps to keep your HVAC system clean. Pets can shed a lot of fur, which can quickly clog up your air filters and reduce the efficiency of your system. Here are five tips to help you keep your HVAC system running smoothly:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

1. Change your air filters regularly<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

This is one of the most important things you can do to keep your HVAC system running efficiently. Depending on the type of filter you have and the number of pets in your home, you may need to change your filters more frequently than the standard three to six months. Check your owner’s manual or talk to your HVAC technician to find out how often you should be changing your filters.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

2. Vacuum around your vents and registers<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Pet hair can easily build up around these areas, so it’s important to vacuum them regularly. This will help ensure that air can flow freely through your vents and registers and prevent pet hair from being recirculated through your home.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

3. Schedule regular professional maintenance<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Having a professional come out to clean and inspect your HVAC system is one of the best ways to keep it running smoothly. They will be able to clean any areas that you might not be able to reach and identify any potential problems before they become serious issues.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

4. Keep your pets groomed<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

The less fur they shed, the less likely it is that their fur will end up in your air filters or ductwork. Regular grooming also helps reduce pet allergies in the home, which can be a major bonus for everyone involved!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

5. Consider an air purifier<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

An air purifier can help remove pet dander from the air, making it easier for everyone in the house to breathe a little easier. Look for an air purifier that uses HEPA filtration for the best results.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Cleaning your HVAC system is important if you live with pets in New York, but it doesn’t have to be an overwhelming task! By following these simple tips, you can help keep your system running smoothly all year long – giving you one less thing to worry about when it comes to taking care of your furry friends!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When to clean your HVAC if living with pets in NY<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

If you live in New York, the best time to clean your HVAC system is in the spring and fall, when the weather is milder and there is less pollen in the air. However, if you have pets that shed a lot, you may need to clean your HVAC system more frequently.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
