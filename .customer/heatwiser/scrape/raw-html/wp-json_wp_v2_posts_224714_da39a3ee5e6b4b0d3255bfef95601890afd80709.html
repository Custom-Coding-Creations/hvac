If you\u2019ve just moved into your new home in Liverpool, chances are, you might not be completely settled in yet. You might be missing some key features in your home, like a HVAC system. Or perhaps you\u2019re already a long-time resident in Liverpool and want to replace your old, outdated HVAC. Well, look no further because you\u2019re on the right page.<\/p>\r\n

Picking the right contractor is important in getting a new HVAC system for your home. Even with a new HVAC system, choosing a reliable, knowledgeable, and experienced HVAC company to take care of maintenance will help extend the life of your unit and keep it performing its best throughout the years.<\/p>\r\n

\u00a0To help you find the right HVAC company for your Liverpool, NY home, here are some tips you should consider:<\/p>\r\n

Conduct extensive research<\/h2>\r\n

Before you even start considering HVAC companies in your locality, you need to do your own research. Create a list of potential candidates and call the Better Business Bureau to check the reputation of each company. It shouldn\u2019t be that hard to find out if a company is doing honest business or just out to con you out of your hard-earned money.<\/p>\r\n

<\/p>\r\n

Try and ask around for referrals<\/h2>\r\n

<\/p>\r\n

Asking for referrals is also another way to find out which companies your friends or neighbors recommend. These referrals will be a great baseline on which companies you should consider, because your friends, family members, or neighbors won\u2019t recommend a company which they think performed poorly.<\/p>\r\n

Read reviews online<\/h2>\r\n

<\/p>\r\n

Check the Better Business Bureau ratings before choosing a contractor. The cheapest quote may not be the best deal if the company has lots of unsatisfied customers. Check their websites for reviews about the services they have already provided. You can also look them up on review websites like Yelp to cross check the authenticity of the reviews posted on their website. These can reassure you that you’ve chosen wisely or steer you away from companies that have poor reviews.<\/p>\r\n

Check if the company is licensed and insured<\/h2>\r\n

<\/p>\r\n

\u00a0Never hire a company that is not licensed and insured. Liverpool, NY requires licensing for HVAC contractors to ensure that every service provider has the experience and proper insurance to protect homeowners. HVAC work is highly technical and requires a level of skill and knowledge to perform well. Don\u2019t be shy to ask about your prospective contractor\u2019s certifications and experience before you hire them.<\/p>\r\n

Look out for rebates and specials, if any<\/h2>\r\n

<\/p>\r\n

\u00a0Check to see if your local utility company offers any rebates or incentives for replacing a defunct HVAC system. You can usually stack offers, so if your state and local utility company both have sales or promotions, take advantage of them. Submit for both to cover a big chunk out of your repair or replacement expenses.<\/p>\r\n

Request a written bid, quote, or estimate<\/h2>\r\n

<\/p>\r\n

Before you say yes to a specific HVAC services company, you should at least look at other quotes to ensure you\u2019re getting the best deal available. It\u2019s important to compare several estimates first before making a decision because prices vary widely from one HVAC company to the next.<\/p>\r\n
