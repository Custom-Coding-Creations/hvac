Institutional & Federal Services

Potter Heating & Air Conditioning-Perrone Plumbing in Syracuse, NY is proud to be partnered with Kondra & Jaquin Enterprises, Inc. With more than 35 years of experience working together, we are their preferred company for all of their heating, cooling, and plumbing needs for corporate and federal projects. We offer the same service with the same level of quality as we do for our residential and commercial customers.

We offer the following services:

  • Air Conditioning
  • Heating
  • Ventilation
  • Insulation
  • Plumbing
  • Industrial Air Handling Equipment

Our Projects Include:

  • Most of our WBE work with Frey & Campbell, Danforth, HT Lyons.
